# NimbusCart — Refund & Returns Policy

## Eligibility window

- Standard items can be returned within **30 days** of delivery.
- Sale items marked *Final Sale* are non-returnable.
- Personal-care items are non-returnable once opened.

## Condition

Items must be returned in their original packaging, unused, and with all
tags attached. We may reduce the refund by up to 20% for items returned
in non-resaleable condition.

## How to start a return

1. Open the order in *Orders → [order id]*.
2. Click *Return or replace items*.
3. Select the reason and print the prepaid return label.
4. Drop the package at any authorized carrier location within 14 days of
   creating the return.

## Refund timing

- Refunds are issued to the **original payment method**.
- Card refunds appear in **5–10 business days** after we receive the
  return at our warehouse.
- PayPal refunds appear within **3 business days**.
- Store credit is issued instantly.

## Damaged or wrong item

If your order arrived damaged or you received the wrong item, we will
ship a replacement at no cost and you do not need to return the original
item. Contact support with photos within 7 days of delivery.

## Non-refundable charges

- Original international shipping fees and customs duties are
  non-refundable.
- Gift wrap and expedited shipping upgrades are non-refundable unless
  the order arrived after the guaranteed delivery date.
