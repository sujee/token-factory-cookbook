# NimbusCart — Shipping Policy

## Domestic shipping (US)

| Tier      | Time              | Cost                          |
| --------- | ----------------- | ----------------------------- |
| Standard  | 3–5 business days | Free over $50, else $4.99     |
| Express   | 1–2 business days | $12.99                        |
| Overnight | Next business day | $24.99 (order before 1pm ET)  |

## International shipping

We ship to 38 countries. Delivery is 7–14 business days depending on
destination. The buyer is responsible for any customs duties or import
taxes — these are collected by the carrier on delivery.

## Order processing

- Orders placed before **1pm ET on a business day** ship the same day.
- Orders placed after 1pm or on weekends ship the next business day.
- During holiday peaks (late November through December) processing may
  take an additional 1–2 business days.

## Lost or stuck shipments

A shipment is considered lost if tracking has not updated for **10
business days** for domestic orders or **21 business days** for
international orders. In those cases we will reship or refund at no
cost — please contact support with your order ID.

## Address changes

Address changes can only be made while the order is in `processing`
status. Once the order is `shipped`, the address is locked and we
cannot reroute the package.
