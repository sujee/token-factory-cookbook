# Research Topic: AI's Evolving Role and Impact on Software Developers

This research aims to understand the actual impact of AI on the software development profession, contrasting early predictions with current industry trends and exploring how AI changes the developer's role, required skills, and learning paradigms.

## Key Questions

1.  What were the prevailing predictions regarding AI's replacement of developers in recent years, and how have these aligned with or diverged from reality?
2.  What are the current hiring trends for software developers in the tech industry, especially among companies that previously underwent layoffs?
3.  What are the recognized limitations of AI in complex software development tasks, particularly concerning human problem-solving, review, security, and maintenance?
4.  How does AI serve as an assistant to developers, and what new paradigms or methodologies (e.g., "build and learn as you go") does it enable?
5.  What fundamental skills are considered most crucial for developers to leverage AI effectively and maintain a competitive advantage in the changing landscape?
