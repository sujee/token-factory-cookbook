# Research Topic: Liquid AI's Open-Source Edge AI Playbook

This research will explore the technical details and industry impact of Liquid AI's open-source playbook for building small, efficient, on-device AI models, specifically within the context of advancements in edge AI.

## Key Questions

1.  What specific challenges does Liquid AI's open-source playbook aim to solve in the development and deployment of small, efficient AI models for edge devices?
2.  How do techniques like hardware-in-the-loop architecture search and specialized distillation objectives contribute to optimizing models for edge efficiency?
3.  What are the applications and technical specifications of Liquid AI's LFM2-VL, LFM2-Audio, and LFM2-ColBERT models?
4.  How does the open-sourcing of such detailed methodologies impact the broader edge AI community and accelerate its development?
5.  What is the evolving paradigm of edge AI, moving from large cloud-based models to distributed, specialized on-device models?
