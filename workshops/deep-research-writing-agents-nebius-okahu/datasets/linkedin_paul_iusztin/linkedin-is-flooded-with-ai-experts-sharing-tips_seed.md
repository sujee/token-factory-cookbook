# Research Topic: Identifying Credible AI Expertise on Social Platforms

Researching the challenges of discerning genuine AI expertise from superficial advice on platforms like LinkedIn, understanding psychological biases that contribute to this phenomenon, and strategies for users to find reliable thought leaders.

## Key Questions

1.  What psychological biases, such as the Dunning-Kruger effect, influence individuals' self-assessment of their expertise, particularly in rapidly evolving fields like AI?
2.  How do genuine experts typically behave and communicate their insights compared to less experienced individuals in professional online settings?
3.  What criteria can social media users employ to critically evaluate the credibility and practical experience of AI professionals sharing content?
4.  What are effective strategies for curating a reliable network of AI thought leaders who prioritize experience and building over algorithmic engagement?
