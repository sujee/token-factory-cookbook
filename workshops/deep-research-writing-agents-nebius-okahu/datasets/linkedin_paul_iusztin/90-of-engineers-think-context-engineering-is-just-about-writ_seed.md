# Research Topic: Comprehensive Context Engineering for LLMs
This research should explore the multifaceted nature of context engineering in Large Language Models, differentiating it from prompt engineering. The goal is to understand how effective context management impacts LLM performance, coherence, and resource utilization.

## Key Questions
1.  What is context engineering, and how does it extend beyond the scope of traditional prompt engineering for LLMs?
2.  What are the distinct components that constitute an LLM's context payload, and what role does each play in guiding model behavior?
3.  How do token limits and dynamic context management mechanisms (like forgetting and retrieving information) influence the design and effectiveness of context engineering strategies?
4.  What are the practical challenges and best practices for curating relevant information to optimize LLM intelligence without introducing noise or exceeding computational constraints?
