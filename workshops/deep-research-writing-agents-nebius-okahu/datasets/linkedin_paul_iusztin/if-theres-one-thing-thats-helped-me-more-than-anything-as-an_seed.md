# Research Topic: Learning Engineering by Building from Scratch

This research will explore pedagogical approaches that emphasize hands-on implementation and "learning with the intention to teach" for engineers, particularly in the context of complex technical domains like AI and machine learning. It will also investigate educational platforms that embody these principles.

## Key Questions

1.  What are the cognitive and practical benefits of "learning by building from scratch" for engineers, especially when dealing with complex abstractions?
2.  How does the intent to teach or explain a concept alter an engineer's learning process and depth of understanding?
3.  What are the core architectural components and functionalities of AI coding assistants, including LLM APIs, tool calling, agent loops, and multi-tool integration?
4.  How do platforms like CodeCrafters.io design educational challenges to guide users through building complex systems from fundamental principles?
5.  What are common corporate policies and procedures for reimbursing employees for professional development courses through L&D budgets?
