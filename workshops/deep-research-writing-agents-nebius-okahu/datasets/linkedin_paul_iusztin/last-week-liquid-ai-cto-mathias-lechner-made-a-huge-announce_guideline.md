# LinkedIn Post Guideline

## Topic
The post should announce a significant open-source playbook detailing how to build efficient, on-device AI models for edge computing.

## Angle
Frame this open-source release as a breakthrough that directly addresses a major bottleneck in edge AI development – the lack of documented best practices for training small models. Emphasize how this transparency will accelerate the entire field towards a future of distributed, specialized edge AI.

## Target Audience
AI/ML engineers, researchers, product managers, and decision-makers working with or interested in edge computing, embedded AI, and model optimization.

## Key Points to Cover
*   Announce a major open-source playbook/report that details how to build small, efficient models for edge devices.
*   Explain the current challenge in edge AI: a knowledge gap for training tiny models, forcing teams to "reinvent the wheel."
*   Highlight how this specific open-source initiative provides the missing documentation and practical techniques.
*   Discuss the strategic importance of this transparency for the future of edge AI, moving towards a distributed model architecture.
*   Include a clear call to action to read the full report.

## Tone
Informative, professional, and forward-looking, with an enthusiastic and slightly provocative edge that positions the announcement as a pivotal moment for the industry.
