# Research Topic: Adapting Clean Architecture for Python AI Projects

This research explores the principles of Clean Architecture and its practical application in Python AI projects, focusing on the challenges of rigid implementations versus flexible, conceptual approaches to achieve maintainable and scalable solutions.

## Key Questions

1.  What are the core tenets of Clean Architecture and related architectural patterns like Hexagonal or Onion Architecture?
2.  How do strict Clean Architecture implementations, often seen in languages like Java, conflict with idiomatic Python development practices?
3.  What are the specific architectural challenges and desired characteristics for maintainable Python-based AI agents and workflows?
4.  How can developers effectively implement architectural boundaries (Domain, Application, Infrastructure, Interface) as conceptual layers rather than rigid folder structures in Python?
5.  What are the benefits of a flexible approach to architecture in Python AI projects regarding reusability, change management, and technical debt?
