# Research Topic: Robust Data Pipeline Architecture for AI Systems

Research best practices and architectural considerations for building high-quality, scalable data pipelines specifically designed to feed AI systems like RAG, agents, and fine-tuned models. This includes understanding the stages from raw data ingestion to consumption, emphasizing data consistency, quality, and maintainability.

## Key Questions

1.  What are the essential architectural layers and components of a scalable data pipeline for AI applications?
2.  How do data collection, extraction, transformation, and loading processes differ when optimizing for AI consumption?
3.  What strategies and techniques are effective for standardizing, cleaning, and ensuring the quality of diverse data sources for LLMs?
4.  Why is decoupling data ingestion from AI processing important, and what are the practical methods for achieving this?
5.  How do data quality metrics and scoring influence the effective use of data in different AI contexts like RAG versus fine-tuning?
