# LinkedIn Post Guideline

## Topic
A personal learning philosophy for engineers and a recommendation for an educational platform that embodies this approach.

## Angle
The post should open with a personal realization about the most effective way engineers learn and master complex topics (e.g., learning to teach, building from scratch). It then transitions to enthusiastically recommending a specific educational platform that perfectly aligns with and facilitates this hands-on, deep-learning philosophy, highlighting a new and relevant project they offer.

## Target Audience
Software engineers, machine learning engineers, and developers who are looking to deepen their understanding of technical concepts and build practical skills, especially in emerging areas like AI.

## Key Points to Cover
*   Start with a strong personal statement about the most impactful learning strategy for engineers (e.g., learning with the intention to teach, building from first principles).
*   Explain how this approach leads to a much deeper understanding of underlying mechanics compared to just using abstractions.
*   Introduce an educational platform that actively promotes this "build from scratch" learning methodology.
*   Highlight a new and relevant project offered by this platform, detailing what users will build and the key concepts they will learn.
*   Conclude with a strong personal recommendation, a clear call to action with an affiliate link, and a helpful tip (e.g., corporate reimbursement).

## Tone
Inspiring, insightful, and practical, sharing a valuable personal lesson while expressing genuine enthusiasm for a recommended solution. It should feel authentic and helpful, rather than overtly salesy, despite containing an affiliate link.
