# LinkedIn Post Guideline

## Topic
The evolving role of developers in the age of AI and why fundamental skills remain crucial.

## Angle
Challenge the common misconception that AI will replace developers. Instead, argue that AI is a powerful assistant that redefines the developer's role, emphasizing problem-solving and foundational skills as the true competitive advantage, creating a "build and learn as you go" paradigm.

## Target Audience
Software developers, engineering managers, and tech professionals curious about the practical impact of AI on the future of programming and career longevity.

## Key Points to Cover
*   Debunk the myth that AI is replacing developers by highlighting current tech hiring trends.
*   Explain that AI capabilities were overestimated; it's a tool that offloads code but creates "hidden taxes" in review, security, and maintenance due to lacking human problem-solving skills.
*   Describe how AI redefines the programmer's role by removing knowledge bottlenecks and lowering the barrier to entry, enabling a "build and learn as you go" approach.
*   Emphasize that strong development fundamentals are now more critical than ever, serving as the developer's competitive advantage with AI.

## Tone
Confident, realistic, and insightful, offering an experienced perspective that is both educational and encouraging. It should be slightly contrarian to common fears, yet pragmatic about AI's limitations and strengths.
