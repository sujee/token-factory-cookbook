# Research Topic: AI Agent Design Patterns and Architectures

Research the different architectural patterns for AI agentic systems, including their core functionalities, advantages, disadvantages, and ideal application scenarios, to understand how to select the most appropriate design for a given problem.

## Key Questions

1.  What are the fundamental characteristics differentiating workflows, pure agents, and hybrid (semi-workflow) systems, and in what contexts is each most suitable?
2.  Explain the ReAct (Reason, Act, Observe) pattern, its typical applications, and the trade-offs involved in its use.
3.  How do Self-Reflective Agents operate to improve output quality and consistency, and for what types of problems are they best suited?
4.  Describe the mechanics, benefits, and risks associated with multi-agent systems such as Networks of Agents (Swarm) and the Supervisor Pattern.
5.  Why is the Hybrid Workflow considered a prevalent pattern for production AI agent systems, detailing its components and advantages?
