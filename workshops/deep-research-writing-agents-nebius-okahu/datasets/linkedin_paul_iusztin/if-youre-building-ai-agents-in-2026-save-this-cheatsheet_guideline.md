# LinkedIn Post Guideline

## Topic
This post should introduce a practical framework or resource that helps AI builders avoid common and costly architectural mistakes when developing AI agent systems.

## Angle
Take a problem-solution approach, starting by highlighting specific, common pitfalls in AI agent architecture that lead to higher costs and complexity. The post should then introduce a valuable, actionable resource (e.g., a cheatsheet or decision guide) designed to help professionals proactively make better architectural decisions from day one.

## Target Audience
AI builders, engineers, developers, and technical teams involved in designing and implementing AI agent systems, particularly those looking to optimize architecture and avoid common errors.

## Key Points to Cover
*   Identify specific, common architectural mistakes made when building AI agent systems (e.g., over-engineering, choosing wrong system types).
*   Explain the negative consequences of these mistakes (e.g., increased cost, latency, debugging difficulty).
*   Introduce a valuable, practical resource (e.g., a cheatsheet, decision framework, guide) designed to address these challenges.
*   Detail the specific benefits and practical insights users will gain from this resource.
*   Provide a clear call to action for users to engage with the post to receive the resource.

## Tone
The tone should be authoritative, helpful, and pragmatic, establishing credibility by identifying common problems and offering a clear, actionable solution. It should convey a sense of urgency regarding the benefits of avoiding architectural missteps.
