# Research Topic: Durable Workflows for Production AI Agents

This research should explore the critical role of durable workflow orchestration tools in productionizing AI agents, contrasting their benefits with those of AI-specific frameworks. It should focus on the technical and operational advantages of prioritizing durability, retry mechanisms, caching, and dynamic execution handling to ensure robust, cost-effective, and scalable AI agent deployments.

## Key Questions

1.  What are the distinct functionalities and limitations of AI frameworks in managing AI agent workflows?
2.  Why is durability crucial for AI agent pipelines, and what are the financial and operational costs of lacking it?
3.  How do modern workflow orchestrators like Prefect enable durable, scalable, and cost-effective AI agent execution, particularly with dynamic workflows?
4.  What are the key differences between traditional orchestrators (e.g., Airflow) and those suited for dynamic AI agents (e.g., Prefect, LangGraph)?
5.  How do durable workflow tools integrate with and complement AI frameworks and infrastructure layers (like Kubernetes) in a comprehensive production AI agent stack?
