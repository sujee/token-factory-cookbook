# Research Topic: Context Optimization Strategies for LLM Agents

Research the critical need for advanced context management in LLM agents beyond basic RAG, focusing on how optimized context impacts agent performance, cost, and reliability. Investigate various techniques and strategies to effectively manage and utilize the LLM's context window.

## Key Questions

1.  What are the key differences between early LLM context handling (e.g., simple RAG) and current context optimization needs for sophisticated agents?
2.  What are the primary challenges and negative consequences (e.g., cost, speed, reliability) of suboptimal context management in LLM-powered agents?
3.  Describe and evaluate the effectiveness of different context optimization strategies, including selection, compression, ordering, isolation, and format optimization.
4.  What specific tools, frameworks, or algorithmic approaches (e.g., RAG + reranking, mem0, MinHash, YAML/XML) are employed within these context management strategies?
5.  How does context ordering (e.g., addressing the "lost-in-the-middle" effect) and context isolation via specialized agents improve LLM reasoning and performance?
