# Branding Profile — Visual Identity

## Color Palette
- **Primary:** Black (#000000) and White (#FFFFFF)
- **Accent:** Orange (#FF6B00) — used for highlights, key elements, and focal points
- **Neutral:** Gray gradients (#333333 → #999999 → #CCCCCC)
- No other colors. Ever. The palette is black, white, gray, and orange only.

## Design Style
- Minimalistic. Clean lines, generous whitespace, no clutter.
- Premium and professional — suitable for LinkedIn feed.
- Abstract and conceptual — represent ideas, not literal scenes.
- Geometric shapes, clean icons, and subtle gradients preferred over photorealistic imagery.

## Composition
- Square orientation 1200x1200 pixels (LinkedIn feed standard).
- Single focal point — one clear visual concept per image.
- No text overlay on the image — the post provides the text.
- No stock photo aesthetics. No corporate handshake imagery.
- No faces or people unless absolutely necessary for the concept.

## Gradients & Effects
- Use black-to-gray or gray-to-white gradients for depth.
- Orange used sparingly as accent — glows, highlights, or key elements.
- Subtle grain or texture is acceptable for a premium feel.
- No drop shadows, no 3D effects, no glossy surfaces.

## Brand Personality in Visuals
- Technical and builder-oriented — think diagrams, architectures, workflows.
- Modern and forward-looking — clean futuristic aesthetics.
- Matches the character profile: real, minimalist, zero hype.
