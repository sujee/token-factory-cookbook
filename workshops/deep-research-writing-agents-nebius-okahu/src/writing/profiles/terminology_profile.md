# Terminology Profile — LinkedIn Posts

## Vocabulary
- Write at a 7-year-old reading level. Simple words, short sentences.
- Casual and direct — like talking to a smart friend, not writing a paper.
- Concrete, hands-on language. Avoid abstract or vague phrasing.

## Voice & Point of View
- Always use active voice. Never passive.
- Use "I" for personal experience and opinions — LinkedIn is personal.
- Use "you" when addressing the reader directly.
- Use imperative verbs for action steps: "Try this.", "Build it.", "Ship it."

## Sentence Style
- Short punchy sentences. 5–20 words each.
- Vary sentence length for rhythm — mix short punches with occasional longer ones.
- Keep transition words minimal. Let line breaks do the work.
- American English.

## Punctuation
- No semicolons or em-dashes. Split into two sentences instead.
- No emojis in the body text. Exception: one emoji in the hook if it adds punch.

## Fluff & Filler
- Zero tolerance for filler sentences that say nothing.
- Every sentence must add information or advance the argument.
- Delete sentences like "In today's rapidly evolving landscape..." or "It's important to note that..."

## Banned AI Slop Expressions
Never use these words or phrases:
- "delve", "tapestry", "vibrant", "landscape", "realm", "embark", "foster"
- "unleash", "beacon", "poised", "unravel", "enrich", "multifaceted"
- "elevate", "resonate", "underscore", "pivotal", "nuanced", "intricate"
- "testament", "paramount", "groundbreaking", "cutting-edge", "game-changer"
- "dive into", "it's important to note", "in today's world"

## Banned Marketing Terms
Never use these words:
- "seamlessly", "significantly", "dramatically", "crucial", "leverage"
- "robust", "scalable", "innovative", "revolutionary", "disruptive"
- "supercharge", "skyrocket", "unlock", "empower", "harness"
- "comprehensive", "holistic", "synergy", "paradigm shift"
