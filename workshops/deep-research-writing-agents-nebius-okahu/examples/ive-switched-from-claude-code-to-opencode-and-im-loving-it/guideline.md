# LinkedIn Post Guideline

## Topic
A personal review and recommendation of a new AI coding assistant tool, comparing it favorably against a well-known alternative.

## Angle
Share a personal, positive experience of switching to a new AI coding assistant, highlighting its technical superiority in agent utilization and open-source nature, while encouraging a community discussion on different tools. Emphasize how a better experience can be achieved even with an existing subscription.

## Target Audience
Software developers, AI engineers, and tech enthusiasts who use or are interested in AI coding assistants and want to discover effective new tools.

## Key Points to Cover
*   Announce a positive personal switch from a popular AI coding tool to a promising new alternative.
*   Detail specific technical improvements or advantages of the new tool, such as better agent orchestration or deeper understanding of complex ideas, contrasting it with the previous tool's limitations.
*   Emphasize the benefit of the new tool being open source, potentially mentioning personal engagement with its code or future plans to explore its mechanics.
*   Mention any delightful user experience aspects, like a polished terminal UI.
*   Conclude with an open question to spark community discussion and gather varied perspectives on similar tools.

## Tone
Enthusiastic, informative, and conversational, reflecting a genuine positive experience while inviting peer engagement and discussion.
